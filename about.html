About Young Chefs

Young Chefs Jamaica’s first competitive reality cooking show featuring children.

In this first season aspiring young chefs ages 8 to 12 years test their skills against each other slicing and dicing their way hopefully to the end of the competition with the hope of being Jamaica’s first "Young Chef".

Each week the chefs will face an exciting new culinary challenge with the winner earning the right to wear the chef’s hat for the next round and being immune from elimination in that round. The last two chefs standing in the kitchen will battle it to the end.

Who will be Jamaica’s first Young Chef? The knives are sharp and the fire is hot. This is serious cooking. Don’t miss it!
