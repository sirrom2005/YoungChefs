Chelsea Stephenson

Chelsea Stephenson's culinary journey began almost 11 years ago when she first enrolled in the Young Chef Summer program.

Though she did not pursue cooking formally she still attended Chef Panton's class's summer after summer perfecting her skills in the kitchen.

She now works with the summer program helping others realise their own passion for all things food.

Chelsea enjoys all cuisine, but if she had to pick a favourite it would definitely be Indian food because of how flavourful it can be.

Currently she attends the University of the West Indies, however still continues working with Young Chef's in the summer months as well as is a Chefs Assistant on the Young Chefs Cooking Challenge.

Joniann Jame

Joniann Jame's culinary career started at the Jose Marti Technical High School where she discoved her love for cooking.

She went on to pursue her career by enrolling in the HEART Commercial Food Preparation level one at the Phillipo Baptist Skills Training Centre and then on to Boy's Town Vocational Training Centre where she completed HEART Food Preparation level two.

Joniann is currently a chef's assistant with Delicious Occasions Cooking Classes and a Commis Chef with Delicious Occasions Catering.
